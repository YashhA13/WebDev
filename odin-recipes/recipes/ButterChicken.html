Butter Chicken

Home

Butter chicken is a type of curry made from chicken cooked in a spiced tomato and butter (makhan)-based gravy. The gravy is typically known for its rich texture. It is similar to chicken tikka masala, which uses a tomato paste.[6] The dish originates in Delhi, India.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Garam Masala
  • Chilli Powder
  • Tomato Paste
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Kasuri Methi

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken for 30 min or more.
  2. Cook chicken in a pan till 80% done, set aside.
  3. In the same pan, heat butter, sauté onions till golden
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook 1 min.
  5. Add tomato puree, chili, turmeric, salt. Cook till oil separates.
  6. Add chicken, cook 5–7 mins.
  7. Add cream, garam masala. Simmer 5 mins.
  8. Garnish with coriander. Serve hot.
