Samosa

Home

Samosas typically consist of a crispy, flaky outer crust made from all-purpose flour, and a flavorful filling, often containing spiced potatoes and peas. Other common ingredients include cumin seeds, ginger, green chilies, and various spices like chili powder, garam masala, and amchur.

Ingredients

  • Cumin seeds
  • Ginger
  • Green chillies
  • Chilli powder
  • Garam masala
  • Amchur

Steps

  1. Make dough: Mix maida, salt, oil, and water. Rest 20 mins.
  2. Prepare filling: In a pan, heat oil, add cumin, then mashed potatoes, spices, salt. Mix well. Cool it.
  3. Roll dough: Make small balls, roll into ovals, cut in half.
  4. Shape samosas: Make a cone with half oval, stuff filling, seal edges
  5. Deep fry on medium flame till golden brown.
